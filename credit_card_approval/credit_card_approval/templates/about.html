Pipeline overview

StepDescription
1. Data sourceapplication_record.csv (demographics & finances) + credit_record.csv (monthly repayment history)
2. LabelingMulti-class STATUS codes (0-5, C, X) collapsed into a binary TARGET: 1 if ever 60+ days past due, else 0
3. Feature engineeringAge and years-employed derived from raw day counts; income-per-family-member ratio computed; missing occupation filled as "Unknown"; EMI paid-off count, EMI pastdue count, and number of loans aggregated per applicant from their monthly credit history
4. EncodingCategorical fields label-encoded; numeric fields standard-scaled
5. ModelingLogistic Regression, Decision Tree, Random Forest, and XGBoost trained with class-imbalance handling
6. SelectionBest model chosen by F1-score on a held-out 20% test split
7. DeploymentBest model + fitted pipeline saved with joblib, served via this Flask app and optionally IBM Watson Machine Learning
